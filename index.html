What do we provide

  • Creating a strong and cohesive brand identity is essential for standing out in a competitive market. At IkinoDesignCo, I offer comprehensive expertise in branding to give your business a distinctive and memorable image.

  • Print is a crucial aspect of your brand strategy. From designing business cards to flyers, posters, and informative brochures, I strive to bring your ideas to life on paper.

  • Your website is often the first point of contact between your brand and potential customers. With a user-centered approach, I create intuitive and aesthetically pleasing websites that enhance your brand image, promote user engagement, and drive conversions.

  • Attractive packaging is crucial for grabbing consumers' attention in a competitive environment. I design innovative and aesthetically pleasing packaging that reflects your brand essence and captivates your target audience at first glance.

  • Social media provides a vast platform to promote and grow your brand. With my expertise in graphic design and content strategy, I create compelling visuals and engaging content that energize your online presence and strengthen your reputation on social platforms. By combining creativity with data analysis, I help you maximize your impact and generate meaningful interactions with your audience.

The Process

  • 01. Inquire

    We have an initial conversation to discuss your needs, ideas, and goals. I take the time to thoroughly understand your vision in order to offer you the best solutions for your project.

  • 02. Planning and Contract

    Once we have established the foundations of your project, we move on to the planning stage. Together, we define deadlines, necessary resources, and project details. Then, we establish an agreement to formalize our collaboration and ensure transparency throughout the process.

  • 03. Design

    It's time to unleash creativity! I work to bring your vision to life through unique and impactful designs. I stay in touch with you throughout the process to keep you informed of progress and gather your feedback.

  • 04. Delivery

    Once you are satisfied with the result, I proceed to finalize the project, ensuring that all details are perfectly in place. Then, I deliver all final files you need to implement your new design.

  • 05. Your brand’s ready to success !!

    And there you have it ! your project is ready to take flight ! With a professional and polished design, your brand is prepared to stand out and captivate your target audience !
